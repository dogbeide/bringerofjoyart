Boyowa means 'Bringer of Joy' in the Itsekiri language of Nigeria.

He started writing poetry, comics, art, and music from a little boy, discovered music production in his early teens. Since then, he has been exploring a variety of genres and art-styles since then such as: singing, songwriting, freestyle rapping, and both visual and media art design.

Healing has been a huge component in his life and has been the main focus ever since he started meditating at age 22.
He is very well experienced with breathwork practices, transformational psychology, and is a certified Reiki Master practitioner with years under his belt.
He has explored Shamanism, has been on an integrative journey with nature to realizing this, and is highly experienced with various forms of subconscious integration, the zero point field, sound healing, and quantum energy work, all using his hands and voice.

Boyowa loves to laugh, live, and joke around. Life is fun! Life is candid, life is a joke (above all, lol!).
